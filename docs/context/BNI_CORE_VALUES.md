# BNI 7 Core Values — Reference

> Source for WM Presenter host-rotation slides. One value featured per week (cycle of 7).
> Slide template: `core_value_{n}` where n = 1..7.

## 1. Givers Gain
By giving business to others, they will want to give business to you. Help others succeed and they will be motivated to help you. Build relationships with other business owners and professionals; offer your expertise unconditionally. You'll be seen as an asset, opening new referral opportunities.

## 2. Building Relationships
Strong, long-lasting relationships are the foundation of business success. Foster genuine connections, have meaningful 1-2-1s, give heartfelt testimonials, share expertise, and mentor newcomers. Be present and active to demonstrate commitment to growing your network.

## 3. Lifelong Learning
Continuously seek new knowledge and stay current with industry trends. Attend BNI trainings, webinars, and LT meetings. Follow BNI on LinkedIn, subscribe to BNI SuccessNet, read industry publications. Investing in professional development makes you a trusted, go-to expert.

## 4. Traditions + Innovation
Balance tried-and-true methods with openness to new ideas. Adapt and evolve business practices; embrace new technologies, tools, and methods. Respecting traditions while embracing innovation builds a business that lasts and attracts referrals.

## 5. Positive Attitude
Approach challenges with optimism and resilience. Practice gratitude, focus on accomplishments, surround yourself with like-minded people. Nobody wants to do business with a negative person. Create an uplifting environment that attracts success.

## 6. Accountability
Be responsible for your actions and follow through on commitments. Set manageable goals, communicate openly with clients and referral partners, always deliver on promises. Accountability builds the trust that drives referrals.

## 7. Recognition
Acknowledge achievements and contributions of others. Celebrate successes of clients, colleagues, and fellow members on social media, with thank-you notes, or shout-outs in chapter meetings. Recognition fosters a culture of mutual support that strengthens relationships.
